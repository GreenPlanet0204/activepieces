title: "Piece Versions"
description: ""
---

All pieces are versioned. When you select a piece from the step picker, it ensures that the flow will not break when the piece is updated, and pieces will not be updated automatically.

To check the current step's piece version, look under the step's settings.
![Piece Versions](/resources/screenshots/piece-version-screenshot.png)

<Tip>
To update a step's piece version, you need to remove the step and add it again.
</Tip>

<Warning>
We plan to streamline the process of upgrading pieces in the future.
</Warning>
